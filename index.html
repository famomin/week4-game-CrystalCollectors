Welcome to Crystal Collector Game

You will be given a random number when you start the game.

There are 4 crystals which holds a random value between 1 and 12. When you click on it it will add that amount to you score.

You goal is to match your score with the number give to you at the start of the game.

You will not see the value of the crystal, until you click on it.

Crystal will hold same value for the game, and will reset when you start a new game.
